About Us

At Hans Up Coffee Shop, we believe in more than just coffee—we believe in creating a warm space where people can connect, relax, and enjoy their favorite brews. Since opening in 2020, we've become a cozy hangout spot for coffee lovers of all kinds.

Whether you're here to fuel your day, catch up with a friend, or simply enjoy the aroma of freshly ground beans, we’ve got the perfect cup waiting for you.

Our History

Hans Up started as a passion project—just one barista with a dream and a lot of coffee beans. From our humble beginnings in a corner café, we’ve grown into a beloved local hub known for great brews and friendly vibes.

Core Values
  • Quality in every cup
  • Sustainability and responsibility
  • Community and connection
  • Creativity and innovation
Our Philosophy

Every customer is family. Every bean has a story. Every visit should feel like home.

Mission

Vision

To become the go-to coffee destination in every neighborhood we serve.
